Top three things to do in Londrina

Coffee Plantations

Visit the scenic coffee fields and learn about Londrina’s rich coffee heritage.

Igapó Lake

Take a relaxing walk or bike ride along Londrina’s famous lakefront park.

Catuaí Shopping

Experience local fashion, food, and entertainment in the region’s largest mall.
